Getting Started

How It Works

Start reading in seconds. No signup, no downloads.

1

Pick Your Level

Choose from A1 (beginner) to B1+ (upper intermediate). Not sure? Start with Book 1.

2

Read the Story

Each sentence shows pinyin with an English translation. Hover to highlight, click to reveal in test mode.

3

Learn the Vocab

Open the vocabulary panel to review key words from each chapter. Build your word bank naturally.

4

Level Up

Move to the next chapter or book when you're ready. The difficulty grows with you.
